Terms
Use Optivra only for images you own or have permission to process. Service availability depends on third-party APIs including OpenAI, Supabase, Stripe, and your WordPress host.

      
Privacy
Optivra stores account details, connected site records, billing references, image job metadata, and uploaded image artifacts required to provide the service. Service role credentials are never exposed to clients.

      
Refund Policy
Subscription changes apply through Stripe billing. Unused credit pack refunds may be reviewed case by case when requested within 14 days and before credits are consumed.
